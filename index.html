Hello, I'm Anna-Maria Christodoulou, a PhD researcher working at the intersection of music and technology. I am currently pursuing my PhD at RITMO, UiO on the topic of Multimodal Information Retrieval for Music Understanding (RITMO Profile).

As a dedicated advocate for reliable, ethical, and accessible Music Information Retrieval (MIR) projects, I strive to make MIR systems open-access. My journey involves not only academic pursuits but also hands-on experimentation with creative MIR applications, including creative music applications.

Beyond my individual endeavors, I take pride in spearheading the Greek MIR Society - MUSAI Gr. Our mission is to bring MIR awareness to the Greek community, bridging the realms of music and computer science for enthusiasts and the general audience alike. From informative posts and basic MIR tutorials to interactive races and engaging paper readings, MUSAI Gr strives to make MIR accessible to everyone.

Feel free to contact me for a collaboration or a chat!
