Combining Music with High-Tech.

Hello, my name is Anna-Maria Christodoulou (or @MuTecEn) and I am an MA in Sound Technology graduate with an IT minor. From January 2023, I am a PhD student at RITMO, UiO, working on Multimodal Music Information Retrieval, using AI. I enjoy working on MIR projects that are not only reliable and ethical, but also accessible and even beneficial to everyone interested in the musical sound. I enjoy attending many MIR-related events and conferences, while I represent and strongly support the female presense in the field.

In my free time, I get my hands dirty by music programming and experimenting with the creative use of MIR. More specifically, I like working on live coding, both creatively() and academically(). I am also a Podcast Producer for CogniHub, implementing intelligent production techniques, using DL.

ABOUT MUSAI Gr: I am the founder and coordinator of the Greek MIR Society - MUSAI Gr. Our main goal is to raise awareness onMIR in the greek community, attracting people with music and/or computer science background, but also the general audience. We aim to achieve our goals, through three main actions: Musai Informative Reposts (weekly posts about MIR trends and updates), Musai Instructive Recordings (basic MIR tasks tutorials), Musai Interactive Races (MIR Capture The Flags) and Musai Interesting Readings (weekly MIR paper readings).

You can find more about my projects on GitHub and you can view some of my original music works on my YouTube channel. Also, feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn.
