Research Question 1

How can multimodality be defined and used in computational music analysis?

Different fields talk about multimodality in different ways. In my PhD, I propose a simple idea: use multiple data sources only when each one adds something new and meaningful to the task. This helps researchers choose the right combination of audio, video, motion, text, and metadata ‚Äî depending on what they want to understand.

Research Question 2

How much can technology enhance the contextual understanding of music?

I worked with several multimodal datasets ‚Äî folk music, classical music, ensemble performance, motion capture, lyrics, and metadata ‚Äî to see whether machines can answer questions like:

  • What is happening in the performance?
  • Why is the performer moving this way?
  • How does culture shape the sound?

These questions require context, not just audio ‚Äî and multimodality helps bring that context into computational models.
